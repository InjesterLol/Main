When an AI agent visits a website, it sees everything — 45,000 tokens of navigation bars, tracking scripts, promotional carousels, and inline CSS. The actual content is 7% of the page. Injester strips the noise and restructures what's left into agent-native data that works on the first try.

What Injester does

Extract

Tavily and Playwright strip scripts, styles, navs, and cookie banners from any URL. 45,280 tokens become 8,540 — clean text with the noise removed.

Optimize

A Nebius LLM restructures the extracted content into agent-native JSON — page intent, typed entities, actionable selectors, key facts. 8,540 tokens become 1,823.

Prove

The Karpathy Loop benchmarks its own output against test questions, identifies failures, rewrites its prompt, and iterates. Three rounds: 3/5 → 4/5 → 5/5. The output earns its score.
